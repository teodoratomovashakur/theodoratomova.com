Teaching

I have taught a core undergraduate course at New York University, Stern School of Business, covering a wide range of organizational behavior and strategy topics, such as structure, leadership, negotiations, teamwork, social networking, among others (class: Management and Organizations; class size: 25 students; ratings: 4.8/5.0; Evaluation Response Rate: 67%).

I have also served as a Teaching Fellow for a variety of classes at NYU Stern such as Negotiation and Consensus Building course (undergraduate-level, master-level, and MBA), Leadership in Organizations course (master-level), and Management and Organizations (undergraduate-level).

As the lead instructor for the Management and Organizations course in January 2020, I was encouraged by the positive evaluations I received from my undergraduate students. Many of my students shared that they were inspired by passion for research and teaching, and described me as a friendly, helpful and knowledgeable instructor.
